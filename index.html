About Us

Light House Institute is a registered ZIMSEC and Cambridge examination center, proudly serving the Zimbabwean community since 1985. Located in Unit J, Zengeza, Chitungwiza, we provide excellent education opportunities structured around the locally and internationally recognized curricula.

Our accredited programs include primary and secondary education, delivered by experienced and dedicated faculty committed to nurturing academic excellence, integrity, and community engagement. We offer comprehensive assessments, preparing students for both ZIMSEC and Cambridge examinations.

Academic Programs and Curriculum

At Light House Institute, we offer a comprehensive curriculum carefully aligned with Zimbabwe’s ZIMSEC syllabus as well as Cambridge International programs. Our academic programs are designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for learning at all levels.

Primary School (Grades 1-7)

Our primary curriculum covers foundational subjects laid out by ZIMSEC:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Shona or Ndebele (Mother tongue language)
  • Environmental Science
  • Religious and Moral Education
  • Physical Education
  • Arts and Crafts
  • Music

Secondary School (Forms 1-6)

Our secondary school curriculum supports both ZIMSEC O-level and A-level subjects including:

  • English Language and Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • History
  • Accounts and Business Studies
  • Information Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Shona / Ndebele Languages
  • Art and Design
  • Physical Education

Each program is designed with continuous assessment and exam preparation to support successful entry into tertiary education and employment. Our dedicated faculty ensure each student receives personalized support.
